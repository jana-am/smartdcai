Steps to get data for Your City
  1. Go to the NREL NSRDB (and/or Wind Toolkit) site and sign in.
  2. Search your location; confirm the coordinates.
  3. Select hourly resolution and your target years.
  4. Check all the attributes below before exporting.
  5. Export as CSV (UTF-8) with header row.
